System Logic

One World, Four Servers

Interconnected Rust cluster. Permanent bases on Main (Tier 1). Temporary 7-day raid structures on Sectors 2-4.

Resource Gating

Tiered Loot Tables

High-density Sulfur, HQM, and T3 Crates are disabled on Main. Players must find Keycards to portal into high-risk resource zones.

Experimental

Ghost Items

High-power "Proto-Weapons" found in Raid Sectors. They provide massive damage but decay and vanish 2 hours after portal transit.

World Events

Monument Sync

Main server resource donations to the "Satellite Dish" trigger Elite Boss events on the Hard Raid server.
