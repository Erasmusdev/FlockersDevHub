HOW IT WORKS

Easy Goal For Ko-fi V2 works by integrating with Streamer.bot or browser sources to track Ko-fi donations in real-time. As donations come in, the blood-bag graphic fills up, providing a visually engaging way to display progress toward your goal. The widget is fully customizable, allowing you to adjust colors, sizes, and animation speeds to fit your stream's aesthetic.
