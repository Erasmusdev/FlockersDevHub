PROFILE: HRZD COLOR-TUNE

HRZD Color-Tune is a lightweight ReShade profile crafted to subtly enhance the visual tone of Horizon Zero Dawn.


This mod focuses on creating a more balanced and natural color palette. The base game can sometimes appear overly bright or saturated. This ReShade aims to correct that by smoothing out intensity for a cleaner, more realistic presentation.
