OVERVIEW

PROFILE: Limansk_Dusk

Limansk_Dusk is a lightweight ReShade profile designed to deepen the post-apocalyptic atmosphere of S.T.A.L.K.E.R. (Any entry in the series).


This mod shifts the color palette towards darker greens, muted browns, and increased contrast to emphasize the dread and grit of LIMANSK. It corrects the sometimes flat lighting of the original engine for a richer, more cinematic feel, ready for any anomaly or mutant encounter.


It also comes with the complementary **Limansk\_Dusk Atmos Shader Preset**, a tailored configuration for the engine's advanced lighting and atmosphere system. This preset is specifically designed to work in tandem with the ReShade profile by focusing on enhancing the **volumetric fog**, **dynamic sky**, and **sun/moon lighting** to leverage the full capabilities of the **X-Ray Engine** or its modded derivatives. The result is a unified visual experience where the grit of the ReShade post-processing meets a deeper, more realistic atmospheric presence from the engine, ensuring optimal visual synergy.
